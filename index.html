Why Council?

I am a Christian, and I firmly believe in loving everyone and being a blessing to other people’s lives. I believe that God has called me to do something big, something that will impact many people. I believe that God will work through me to show everyone how real and awesome God is. This is really a motivation for me to join council, as I want to grow as a leader so that I will be able to do more things for other people and to let people realise who and what God is.
Another reason is because I want to grow as a person, into a more confident person who people can rely on. I want to do my best for the school. I want to be known as a person who cares for people rather than a person who did well for his studies. Sure studies are important, but I value people more than studies.
Lastly, I joined council because many people encouraged me to join. If I didn’t try to join council, I would regret it and I might have let them down. I don’t want any of that.
